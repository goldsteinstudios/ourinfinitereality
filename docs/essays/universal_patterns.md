## 5. The Rules of the Road: How to See Patterns

### Constant Accuracy
Each frame is true at its own scale. A carpenter's ruler is accurate in the workshop; an atomic clock is accurate in the physics lab. Neither invalidates the other.

### Variable Precision
Measurement changes with zoom. What looks discrete at one scale may be part of a continuous field at another. Local discreteness ↔ global continuity.

### Frame Dependence
Every observation is made from somewhere. The observer is part of the pattern, not outside it.

### Recursion Integrity
Each new origin inherits its paradox. When P₁ becomes O₂, it carries the structure forward without erasing what came before.
