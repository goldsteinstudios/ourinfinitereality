# The Text

*An ancient field guide, decoded*

---

You came through the door marked "Text." Welcome.

The Dao De Jing isn't philosophy. It's documentation. 2,400 years ago, observers noticed the same pattern we've been tracing through physics and biology—and they wrote it down. Not as mystical poetry, but as structural notation. The Chinese characters themselves are diagrams.

This section contains structural translations of the chapters we've completed, plus the lexicon that decodes how the characters work.
