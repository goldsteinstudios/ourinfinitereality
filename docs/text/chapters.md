# The Chapters

*Enter anywhere. Each one opens onto the whole.*

---

## Core Structure

The chapters that establish the coordinate system:

- **[Chapter 1 — The Coordinate System](../translations/chapters/chapter01_2025-12-07.md)**
  Where everything begins. The two axes. The paradoxical center.

- **[Chapter 11 — Void and Function](../translations/chapters/chapter11_2025-11-27.md)**
  The wheel, the pot, the room. Function IS the emptiness.

- **[Chapter 25 — Spherical Recursion](../translations/chapters/chapter25_sphere_proof_2025-12-01.md)** ✓ Guodian
  The full recursive cycle, explicitly stated. 大→逝→遠→反.

- **[Chapter 42 — Dimensional Proof](../translations/chapters/chapter42_2025-11-26.md)**
  道生一，一生二，二生三，三生萬物. How dimensions emerge.

---

## Pattern Dynamics

How the pattern moves:

- **[Chapter 40 — The Oscillation Engine](../translations/chapters/chapter40_2025-11-26.md)** ✓ Guodian
  反者道之動. Reversal is the movement of pattern.

- **[Chapter 2 — Co-emergence](../translations/chapters/chapter02_2025-11-26.md)** ✓ Guodian
  有無相生. How opposites arise together.

- **[Chapter 16 — Return to Root](../translations/chapters/chapter16_2025-11-26.md)** ✓ Guodian
  歸根曰靜. The 知常 cascade.

- **[Chapter 22 — The Arc Operation](../translations/chapters/chapter22_2025-11-26.md)**
  曲則全. Curved, then complete.

---

## Observation & Frame

Different ways of seeing:

- **[Chapter 41 — Frame Responses](../translations/chapters/chapter41_2025-11-26.md)** ✓ Guodian
  上士聞道. How different frames respond to pattern.

- **[Chapter 52 — Return to Source](../translations/chapters/chapter52_2025-11-26.md)** ✓ Guodian
  天下有始. The mother and the children.

- **[Chapter 56 — Mysterious Sameness](../translations/chapters/chapter56_2025-11-26.md)** ✓ Guodian
  知者不言. Knowing and speaking as orthogonal operations.

---

## Alignment (德)

Straight path through internal state space:

- **[Chapter 38 — Alignment Degradation](../translations/chapters/chapter38_2025-11-26.md)**
  上德不德. How alignment degrades through layers.

- **[Chapter 51 — Pattern Nurtures](../translations/chapters/chapter51_2025-11-26.md)**
  道生之，德畜之. Pattern generates; alignment nurtures.

- **[Chapter 21 — Pattern-Alignment](../translations/chapters/chapter21_2025-11-26.md)**
  孔德之容. The shape of profound alignment.

---

## Water & Yielding

The paradigm of aligned action:

- **[Chapter 8 — Water](../translations/chapters/chapter08_2025-11-26.md)**
  上善若水. The highest good is like water.

- **[Chapter 78 — Water Overcomes](../translations/chapters/chapter78_2025-11-26.md)**
  天下莫柔弱於水. Nothing softer overcomes harder.

- **[Chapter 76 — Soft and Hard](../translations/chapters/chapter76_2025-11-26.md)**
  人之生也柔弱. The living are soft; the dead are hard.
